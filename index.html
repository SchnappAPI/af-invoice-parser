AppFolio Invoice Parser
1
Drag the button above to your bookmarks toolbar. Or right-click it → Bookmark This Link → Bookmarks Toolbar.
2
Go to AppFolio and click the bookmarklet. The parser panel opens at the bottom of the page.
3
Go to the Upload CSV tab, drop your AppFolio report CSV, enter your API key, click Run batch.
4
The page navigates through each invoice automatically. Watch live progress in the log. Do not click away while running.
5
When complete, edit any cells in the results table, then Export CSV or Export Excel.
Processing is sequential — one invoice at a time in this tab. You will see the page navigate to each invoice, fetch the attachment, extract data with Claude, and return. Pay mismatches (Amount Due vs Paid) are flagged in red automatically.
